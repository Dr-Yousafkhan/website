Meet Dr. Yousaf

With an unwavering commitment to patient care and a deep expertise in physiotherapy, Dr. Yousaf Khan Niazi provides comprehensive rehabilitation services that prioritize individual wellness and recovery. A distinguished graduate of the University of Management and Technology, where he earned a Doctor of Physiotherapy degree with multiple academic honors, Dr. Yousaf has gained extensive hands-on experience in renowned hospitals and clinics, including Central Park Medical Teaching Hospital, Surayya Azeem Hospital, and Mumtaz Bakhtawar Memorial Trust Hospital

Dr. Yousaf is skilled in a variety of therapeutic techniques, from manual therapy and therapeutic exercises to dry-needling
acupuncture. His treatment approach is grounded in evidence-based practices, tailored rehabilitation plans
compassionate care that helps each patient regain mobility, strength, and quality of life. Through his commitment to
continuous learning and excellence in care, Dr. Yousaf aims to be a trusted partner in his patients' journey toward
health and wellness.
