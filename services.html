Comprehensive Physiotherapy Services

At Dr. Yousaf Khan Niazi Physiotherapy Practice, we are dedicated to providing a range of specialized services designed to support each patient's unique path to recovery.Our evidence-based treatments are crafted to relieve pain, restore movement, and enhance overall wellness. Each service is delivered with the utmost care, professionalism, and a focus on lasting outcomes.

  • Frozen shoulder: Personalized hands-on treatments to relieve pain, restore joint function, and improve mobility.
  • Scatica: Guided exercises tailored to strengthen muscles, improve flexibility, and support full recovery.
  • Back pain: Advanced treatments using therapeutic currents to accelerate healing and reduce pain.
  • Post surgery rehabilitation: Techniques fasten the natural healing responses.
  • Home Physiotherapy available: Comprehensive in-home services for patients who prefer or require treatment
    in the comfort of their homes.

With Dr. Yousaf's expertise and commitment, each patient receives personalized care that
promotes faster recovery, prevention of future injuries, and a return to an active, healthy lifestyle.
